With Modern Money you are 100% secured

Modern Money is a multisignature wallet, which is like a safe with two keys.

You have one key, and Lykke has the other. No one can take your money, even if your phone is stolen.

Every transaction is approved by fingerprint and for additional security, we ask for PIN code as well.
