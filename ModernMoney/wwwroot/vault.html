Your personal Modern Money Vault is protected by additional layers of security. From your Vault to your wallet

How do you retrieve money from the Vault?

For additional security, we have put in place a time lock for transfers of funds from your Modern Money Vault to your personal wallet. The time lock is a 24-hour period during which the Vault is prevented from executing a requested transfer of funds. An email confirmation is also required to begin the time lock period.

Conversely, you can always transfer money from your Portfolio to your personal Vault or receive direct external transfers to your Vault when you want to save. Modern Money Vault supports the same Portfolio as your standard account, and enables you to save any amount in any currency you want.
