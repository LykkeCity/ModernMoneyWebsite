Discover the technology

Discover the technology

The Lykke marketplaces uses the distributed ledger, which is blockchain technology pioneered by Bitcoin. A blockchain is a sequence of linked data records, or blocks. Each block refers to an earlier block in the chain, which strengthens the security of the entire sequence and makes it possible to store the data in a decentralized way, across mu ltiple network-enabled devices in physical locations anywhere in the world. Using the blockchain as a ledger for financial transactions—as Bitcoin does, and as Lykke does—all but eliminates the risk of fraud, because the ledger exists in no fixed place. Rather, it exists in multiple places simultaneously, beyond the reach of any single authority.
