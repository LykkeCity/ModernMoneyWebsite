Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Modern Money Prepaid Card and where can I use it?

    You can think of the Modern Money Prepaid Card as a more modern version of the standard debit cards. It is accepted everywhere where you can pay with a normal prepaid card, but with Modern Money, you have full control over your card, and you choose what currencies to spend with it.

  • How do I get my Modern Money Prepaid Card?

    You can request your Modern Money Prepaid Card using your Modern Money application. Your card can be shipped in a couple of days after you request depending on your location.

  • How do I add money to my card?

    After you have added money to your Modern Money through a bank or credit card transfer, just go to the Modern Money Prepaid Card page and link currency to your card.
